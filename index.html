Work packages

SWIFT-HEP is organised in 5 work packages:
  1. Intelligent data and workflow management(WP1)
  2. Event generators(WP2)
  3. Simulation(WP3)
  4. Reconstruction and trigger(WP4)
  5. Analysis Systems(WP5)
  6. Training

Meetings

Meetings are held on a monthly basis to focus on a specific topic. They are typicallone-hour long on a Friday afternoon. Conveners of the 5 work packages meet every 4 weeks to plan future work See the Indico category for full details.
Workshops are organised every six months for the community to get together.

Activities

A C++ training is planned in 2022. This will follow the SIDIS curriculum. Details to follow soon. A proposal to participate to the NVIDIA hackathon is being submitted by the Optix team.
SWIFT-HEP is a project funded by STFC to contribute to the development of software for High Energy Physics experiments.
