SWIFT-HEP is a project funded by STFC to contribute to the development of software for High Energy Physics experiments.

Work packages

SWIFT-HEP is organised in 5 work packages:
  1. Intellingent data and workflow management
  2. Event generators
  3. Simulation
  4. Reconstruction and trigger
  5. Analysis systems

Meetings

Meetings are held on a monthly basis to focus on a specific topic. They are typically one-hour long on a Friday afternoon.
Conveners of the 5 work packages meet every 4 weeks to plan future work
See the Indico category for full details
Workshops are organised every six months for the community to get together.
